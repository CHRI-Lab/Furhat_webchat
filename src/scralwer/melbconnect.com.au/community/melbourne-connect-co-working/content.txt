Be immersed within an innovation precinct and be part of a connected community.
Melbourne Connect Co-working is a dynamic community of innovators. Situated at the heart of Australia’s leading innovation precinct, Melbourne Connect Co-working aims to bring together collaborators whose work aims to positively impact the world. 
Located over two levels at Melbourne Connect and stretching across 2,200 square-metres, this unique space offers a selection of flexible private suites, dedicated desks, meeting rooms, project spaces, media studios and event spaces to be a creative home for a curated community of start-ups, scale-ups, and individuals looking to be immersed within a digital innovation ecosystem.
