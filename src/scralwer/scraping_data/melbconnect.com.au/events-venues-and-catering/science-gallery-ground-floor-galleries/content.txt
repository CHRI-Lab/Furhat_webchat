Science Gallery, Ground Floor Galleries
Science Gallery Melbourne is located on the Ground Floor of Melbourne Connect. The Ground Floor houses lofty gallery spaces on the east and west side of the building, which are connected via an entrance foyer and café area. With natural light spilling in through the floor to ceiling windows on all sides, the Ground Floor Galleries provide versatile and flexible spaces for events, such as intimate cocktail and dinner style events for 20-50 people as well as stand up functions for up to 1,000. Science Gallery also hosts two flagship exhibitions per year, which are available for private viewings as part of your event. 
Availability
Daytime: 8am-4pm
Evening: 6pm – 11pm

Capacity
Cocktail: Up to 300 across whole ground floor, with options to service smaller events in sectioned-off areas. 
Dining: Up to 50 
























How to Get There
The Ground Floor Gallery is accessible from the Grattan Street entry into Science Gallery Melbourne. Proceed from Grattan Street through the glass door entry and make a left turn toward the Ground Floor Gallery.
